Case Study Structure

1. Hero
   - Category
   - Title
   - One-line Summary

2. Hero Image

3. Challenge

4. Insight

5. Approach

6. Visual Showcase
   - 1–3 image blocks

7. Outcome

8. Deliverables

9. Next Project

Guidelines

- 5–7 images maximum
- 4–6 content sections maximum
- Keep copy concise
- Focus on thinking, not process
- Every case study should be readable in 2–3 minutes
